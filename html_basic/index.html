Women In Tech Network Event

Uganda Innovation Week

by Angel Wanyama

Last Week, in collaboration with Mkazipreneur, we hosted a Women In Tech Network event at the National ICT Innovation HUB, Nakawa, as part of the Uganda Innovation Week.

Our founder and Managing Director, Barbara Mutabazi, kicked off the event with a powerful keynote, urging entrepreneurs to embrace the digital economy and expressing gratitude to the Uganda Communications Commission for empowering women with digital skills nationwide.

The event's highlight was a panel discussion moderated by Patricia Kahill, featuring accomplished #WomenInStem and entreprenuers;

List of panelists

  1. Martha Tukei, Head of Enterprise Business at Airtel Money, Airtel Uganda Limited
  2. Renita Nabisubi, Lead Digital Economy, Kampala, Mastercard Foundation
  3. Julian Rweju, Manager Business Transformation at NITA(U)
  4. Vaolah Amumpaire, Founder & CEO of Wena
  5. Tr Basemera Stella Maris, Founder at Creative Learning Africa+

The panelists explored opportunities for Ugandan female entrepreneurs in the digital economy. In summary, they emphasized the importance of legal support for ventures to thrive globally, the significance of reaching beyond local communities, and the need for fostering peer learning spaces to encourage innovation and marketing of truly exceptional products.
